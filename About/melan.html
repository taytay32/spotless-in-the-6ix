MÉLANIE

Mélanie was born and raised in the quaint and quiet east coast fishing town of Beresford, NB. After completing her degree at Ottawa U, she packed up the truck and set sail for Toronto, alongside her favourite canine Mika.

Mélanie is a perfectionist and cares deeply for the people around her. In January 2017, she launched Spotless In The 6ix, combining her passion for cleaning with her desire to help make a difference. Spotless is Mélanie’s way of contributing to a healthy community by creating clean and safe homes for her clients, giving them back the time they need for the important things in life.

Want to know more? Shoot her an email at spotlessinthe6ix@gmail.com.
